Alexander Wiebe,

AKA AJ was born on January 12, “1987” with a programmable calculator in one hand and a solved rubik’s cube in the other. With roots in QBasic he’s been coding ever since. Born and raised in the prairies AJ is a local grad from U of R. While he loves his wife and daughter; JavaScript is his passion.

AJ’s experience involves N-Tier Enterprise apps, rewriting a rowing machine’s driver in typescript, and everything in between.
